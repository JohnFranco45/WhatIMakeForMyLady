Garlic Shrimp

Whether it's a sunny or rainy outside, our creamy Garlic Shrimp is perfect for your date night. you can either serve it with white rice or pasta either or your lady will love it!

    Ingredients

  • 1 LB Shrimp
  • 10 Cloves of Garlic (She LOVES garlic)
  • 2 ounces Chili Oil
  • 3 ounces Kewpie Mayo
  • 1 Tablespoon Chili Flakes
  • 1 ounce Oil
  • 2 ounces Heavy Cream

If you love garlic then you're in for a treat, you'll love this recipe. Great for appetizers, gathering of all sizes, easy to make and full of flavor.

Yum!!!

Preparation

  1. Devein the shrimp and take the shell off. Now you have the option of keeping the head of the shrimp or removing it and if you keep the head to marinate with the rest of the ingredients. I promise you, its a free ticket to flavor town.
  2. Make sure the shrimps are clean and strain all the water. Make sure there no excess water, we want most flavor for our lady right? Right???
  3. Mince your garlic and combine all ingredients in a bowl and once everything is nicely mixed add the shrimps.

Lets Cook!

  1. In a hot pan add in your oil.
  2. Add your mince garlic, wait until its golden brown.
  3. Add your shrimp and sear each side for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the remaining marinate and let it simmer for 1 minutes.
  5. Add the heavy cream and a pinch of salt.

Enjoy!
